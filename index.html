AI-powered Alt Text Generator for Blind People

Enhancing Museum Experiences for Visually Impaired Visitors

Project Overview

The AI-powered Alt Text Generator aims to transform museum experiences for visually impaired visitors by providing detailed, meaningful descriptions of artwork and exhibits. Our solution leverages artificial intelligence to generate rich, contextual descriptions that go beyond basic visual elements to include historical context, artistic techniques, and emotional aspects of the artwork.

Problem Statement

Museums are inherently visual spaces that often present accessibility challenges for blind and visually impaired visitors. Traditional solutions like pre-recorded descriptions or braille text are limited in their effectiveness and require significant planning ahead, preventing spontaneous museum visits.

Our Solution

Our solution is an AI-powered tool that generates descriptive alt text for artwork in real-time. Instead of maintaining a database of pre-written descriptions, we harness the power of AI to create detailed art descriptions on demand, enabling visually impaired visitors to experience museums with greater autonomy and engagement.

Key features include:

  • Real-time generation of detailed artwork descriptions
  • Integration with standard accessibility tools used by visually impaired individuals
  • Guided photo-taking using audio cues for optimal image capture
  • Multisensory descriptions that go beyond visual elements
  • Support for spontaneous museum visits without prior planning

Needfinding Process

Our needfinding process involved extensive research into the challenges faced by visually impaired museum visitors, interviews with accessibility coordinators, and analysis of first-person accounts from blind individuals about their museum experiences.

Research Methodology

  • Interviews with Fine Arts Center (FAC) staff
  • Literature review of academic papers on museum accessibility
  • Analysis of first-person accounts from blind and visually impaired museum visitors
  • Affinity diagramming to identify key themes and pain points
